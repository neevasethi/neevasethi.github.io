Intro

With everything I do, my main goal is to leave a positive impact on other people, and augment society. I wish to leave a deep impact on people’s lives, and have the work that I do contribute to the greater good of humanity; I love learning how to use code to make our world a better place. The widespread impact that technology has in this day and age is something we have never experience before, and as computer scientists, it is our job to create the technology that will make tomorrow better.

I currently have a large range of technical skills, but as I am still a student, I am always wiling and eager to learn the technical skill necessary to work on projects that make a difference.

At school I am a very involved in extracirriculars as I believe they expose you to experiences and knowledge that you cannot get within a classroom setting.

I am currently the Vice President of Dream Team Engineering: A student-run organization that created novel technologies for the children at Shands hospital, a Care Coordinator for the Mobile Outreach Clinic: A student-run, free mobile clinic the provides healthcare to the underserved community with Alachua county, an undergraduate researcher in Dr.Tucker's Health Disparities and Health Promotion Research lab, and a volunteer for the PCICU and PICU at Shands Hospital. More details can be found under the "Work" tab.

Many of these activities have nothing to do with computer science and coding, so you may be wondering why I am involved with them. The answer goes back to what I stated above: my main goal is to leave a postive impact on people's lives and augment society. With any of the extra time that I have, I love to be hands-on with my community and postiviely leave an impact on the community around me. Each of these extracirriculars allows me to go out there and make the community a genuinely better, happier place, and at the end of the day, that is how I find fulfillment
